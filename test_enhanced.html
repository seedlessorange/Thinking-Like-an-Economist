Analyze the impact of an oil workers' strike on the oil market. What happens to price and quantity?
Answer & Explanation

Analysis: The shutdown reduces supply. The supply curve shifts left, driving quantity down and prices up.

Economic Reasoning:

  1. Strike reduces production capacity → Supply decreases
  2. Supply curve shifts from S₀ to S₁ (leftward)
  3. New equilibrium: Lower quantity (Q₀ → Q₁), Higher price (P₀ → P₁)
