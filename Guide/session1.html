Why Think Like an Economist?

1. Definition: What is an Economy?
Economy Definition

An economy is a system where people and organizations create, exchange, and distribute scarce resources under a set of rules. Economic actors (people supplying, people demanding) exchange products or services for value, aiming to not be commoditized.

Trading products & services between buyers and sellers
