Key Insight: The Competition-Profit Trade-off

As we move from left to right across the table (perfect competition ‚Üí monopoly):

  • Competition decreases (‚àû firms ‚Üí 1 firm)
  • Market power increases (price taker ‚Üí price maker)
  • Price-cost margin grows (P = MC ‚Üí P >> MC)
  • Long-run profits increase (zero ‚Üí positive)
  • Allocative efficiency decreases (efficient ‚Üí deadweight loss)

Remember: Real markets rarely fit perfectly into one category. Most markets exist somewhere along this spectrum, with characteristics from multiple structures.
