Why De Beers Restricts Diamond Supply

By producing only 200 diamonds instead of the competitive quantity of 400, De Beers:

  • Charges $600 instead of $200 per diamond
  • Earns $80,000 profit instead of zero
  • Creates artificial scarcity with the slogan "A Diamond is Forever"
  • Maintains high prices by controlling 85%+ of world diamond supply

This is why monopolies are inefficient: they restrict output below the socially optimal level to maximize their own profit.
